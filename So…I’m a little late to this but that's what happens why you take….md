Back in May, God gave me the pleasure to go study abroad in Costa Rica and work on this reconstruction project in a beautiful small community of Turrialba. I got to learn and experience so much of the culture of Costa Rica. They were so warm and inviting and everyone knew each other. It exposed me to how much I truly miss the sense of community and the lack of it, here, in the United States. While it's fun to go vacation down in Costa Rica, God gave me a mission: and that was to learn. Whether that be from learning construction tools and engineering, how to be patient when things go our way, and most importantly how to put the word human back into humanity. I walked with skills and experience but with lessons that taught me that life is more than just you.

Life is to serve others and yourself in them. To be equipped so you can solve problems. The best leaders are the ones who know that they are ultimately the servant. That's what Jesus did and that's what I'm going to continue to do.
