# Joshua 24:15

Faith starts at home! If you don’t set the tone of prayer at home, then you miss out of what God is trying to tell you.

Who you are anywhere, is who you are everywhere

Am I setting the tone at home when life doesn’t propel me to.

There must be a leadership of prayer set in the home.

People rarely rise higher than the spiritual example they see daily.

When God has a plan for the household so does the enemy

Prayer points
1. Christ centered household
2. Developing a passion for Christ
3. Unity
4. Needs of the house

The enemy is loud but your prayer must be **louder**
