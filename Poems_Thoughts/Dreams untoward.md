On a glistening night, entering the sound comfort of my bed. I turn to my side waiting for the other side to meet me. There I must meet the other me in whence I have been accustomed to. There I see the white light on the horizon greeting me with such great enthusiasm. I reach out to touch in hopes to grasp a crumb of its glory. But it disappears underneath the seams of my palm. I walk toward an untoward light that captures the attention of my eye. I seek to find what may be of this. Could it be the horizon leading me another way? I seek beyond of the realm of my steps and follow my heart desire to seek what I know is greater than I. I marched onward to a place I cannot seem to perceive but yet I wait among the bewildered environment to see if the answers finds me instead. I wait. And I wait. And there it is, a beauty that seems to meet me so gently. It’s an aroma that makes you think that life is worth little but to die is worth much. On the other side, could much more be waiting for me after. I start to begin the journey towards but it collapses into pieces of the sky. What was now a full, beautiful picture of buoyancy. Now has decided to leave. What was it? I don’t know. But my spirit is waiting for greeting once again.
